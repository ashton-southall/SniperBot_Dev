SniperBot

SniperBot is an Advanced Moderation Bot for Twitch and Discord that utilizes Artificial Intelligence to make Moderation Decisions,
SniperBot is capable of timing out “wanna become famous” bots on twitch and deleting known troll messages with configurable blocked message thresholds.
It also features a global blacklist, this is for the worst offenders on both Twitch and Discord timing out a blacklisted chatter on all channels SniperBot moderates on twitch,
and kicking all blacklisted Discord users from servers SniperBot moderates.

Currently SniperBot collects basic information such as chat messages to be read by an external AI,
the AI responds with the intent of every message sent in twitch channels using SniperBot and SniperBot will carry out actions depending on those messages and the channel’s moderation settings.

Note: Some features talked about may not be added to the public bot yet, these features are coming soon.
