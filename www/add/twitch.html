Let's Get You Setup!

To invite SniperBot to your twitch channel simply go to SniperBot's twitch channel and send the command !sniperbot join

Now that you've done that the rest is simple!

Back to your channel now, add sniperbotontwitch as a moderator of your channel with /mod sniperbotontwitch

You're all done! SniperBot is a set and forget bot meaning you only need to setup the bot and everything else is done for you
